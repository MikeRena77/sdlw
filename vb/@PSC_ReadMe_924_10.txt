Title: _ Reverb :: Playlist generation program :: ASX M3U WPL 3mP PLS HTML TXT
Description: This program goes though your MP3 collection, creating playlists automatically in the format you choose for artists you have 5 or more songs from.
Can also create one large playlist from all your songs...
Supports playlist types: ASX M3U WPL 3mP PLS HTML TXT
Also see the website for EXE / screenshots etc: http://thecleaner.publication.org.uk/reverb
Enjoy!
